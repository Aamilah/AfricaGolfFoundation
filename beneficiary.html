Welcome to the Africa Golf Foundation

Become a Beneficiary

Beneficiaries receive top-notch training, participate in competitive tournaments, and enjoy mentorship from experienced golfers. Additionally, they benefit from scholarships, financial aid, and opportunities for personal development, all while being part of an initiative that values environmental stewardship and inclusivity.

How to Become a Beneficiary?

The Africa Golf Foundation offers a variety of events services to support individuals in the golfing community.

Find a charity event.

A golf charity event or organisation that supports a cause you care about. Contact us to learn about the AFG.

Register for event.

Register to join the event as a partner or member of a team, and learn more about the charity missions.

Donate to the cause.

Raise funds or donate, through the event's fundraising events, by participating and enjoying the event.

Support other events.

For all events that you participate in the contributions made help support the charity's mission.
