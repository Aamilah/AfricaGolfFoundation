Changing Lives, Creating Opportunities, Empowering Communities One Swing At a Time

We are dedicated to changing lives through the power of golf, creating opportunities that empower communities and foster inclusive growth. Our mission is to make the sport accessible to all, driving positive change across the continent.

About Us

The Africa Golf Foundation (AGF) is an organization dedicated to promoting and developing the sport of golf across Africa by increasing accessibility to golf and fostering a vibrant golfing community. Our goals go beyond the sport as we are dedicated not just to grow the sport but to harness the power of the sport to bring real and sustainable change and empower communities.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to grow the sport of golf across Africa by fostering inclusivity and increasing accessibility for all. We are committed to going beyond the game, empowering communities by creating opportunities and creating real and sustainable change.

How We Serve

We are dedicated to fostering real and sustainable change through the power of golf. We are dedicated to breaking down barriers and promoting inclusivity through the following methods:

Grants and Scholarships

Through our grants and sponsorships, we provide support to talented individuals from previously marginalised backgrounds, enabling them to pursue their dreams in diverse roles in the industry. Whether aspiring to be players, coaches, caddies, groundskeepers, or any other occupation in golf, we offer funding for training, education, travel and exposure and other essential requirements. Our goal is to empower individuals with the resources they need to thrive and succeed in their chosen path within the world of golf, fostering diversity and enriching the sport for all.

Read More

Training and Development

Through our training and development program, we offer a range of training opportunities, delivered both directly and through trusted partners, designed to enhance the knowledge and abilities of individuals and organisations involved in golf across Africa. Our program includes training camps, clinics, seminars, outreach initiatives and sponsorship for coaching and education. Our programs are not limited to the sport alone but also to developing the individual holistically including managing their income, image and other related issues to maximise their potential. Through these efforts, we aim to cultivate a skilled and empowered golf community in Africa, driving growth, fostering talent and ensuring the sport's enduring success for generations to come.

Read More

Mentorship Programs

We believe in the power of mentorship to unlock potential and create opportunities for individuals who have previously had barriers to access the sport. Through our mentoring program, we match aspiring individuals to seasoned veterans who provide guidance, support, and expertise to maximise their growth and opportunities, both on and off the course. Our mentors assist in honing skills, navigating career paths , and fostering personal development, ensuring that each mentee has the tools and support needed to succeed in the world of golf and in life in general.

Read More

Community Outreach

We are committed to expanding access to golf and creating opportunities for communities that have been underserved historically. Through our community outreach program, we actively engage with areas that have little to no exposure to golf, raising awareness about the sport's potential and the opportunities that it offers. Our outreach efforts include coaching camps and clinics, school visits, community trips to golf clubs and access to tournaments, and speaking engagements by established members of the golf community. By providing these experiences and resources, we aim to inspire and empower young people from marginalised communities , fostering love for the sport and opening doors to new possibilities.

Read More

Equipment Donations

We regularly organise donation drives to collect both new and used golf equipment. These donations serve a dual purpose: first, they enable us to expand our community outreach efforts by providing equipment for training clinics, camps and other programs that introduce golf to individuals who may have never had access to the sport. Second, we use the donations to support talented individuals who face financial barriers. By redistributing donated equipment to those in need, we ensure that everyone has the opportunity to pursue their golfing dreams, regardless of financial circumstances.

Read More

Supporting Golf Clubs

We are dedicated to strengthening golf clubs and facilities by providing vital resources and support. We offer training for officials and administrators, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to effectively manage and oversea club operations. We also offer training and equipment for groundskeepers, ensuring that courses are maintained to the highest standards. We assist clubs in organising and hosting tournaments and events , fostering a vibrant community. Africa Golf Foundation also has a twinning program with international golf clubs, allowing for the exchange of best practices and collaboration to create world-class facilities.

Improving Governance

We recognise the importance of good governance in fostering growth and development of golf across Africa which is why we are committed to collaborating, with governing bodies to improve governance practices within the sport. Through our partnership initiatives, we will work closely with governing bodies to provide training , resources and support aimed at enhancing transparency, accountability and efficiency in golf administration. By promoting principles of good governance such as integrity, inclusivity and ethical leadership, we aim to strengthen the foundation of the sport and to ensure its long-term sustainability . Together with our partners, we will strive to create a culture of excellence and professionalism within the golf community to the benefit of players and enthusiasts across the continent.

Financial barriers should never stand in the way of talent and passion. That’s why we offer grants, sponsorship and scholarships to individuals across Africa, providing them with resources to they need to pursue their golfing dreams. Whether its funding for equipment, coaching or tournament participation, we are here to support the next generation of golfing stars

Environmental Protection and Sustainability

Africa is facing one of its greatest challenges; global warming. We are dedicated to helping to tackling the challenge by working with golf clubs and other partners to improve the environment. We will partner with tournament organisers to use sustainable methods and with golf clubs to help maintain the golf courses as nature reserves.

Africa Golf Foundation Events

The Africa Golf Foundation will launch a series of regular annual events to promote the growth of the sport on the continent while assisting in developing our talented golfers. We will seek partnerships with other members of the golf fraternity in creating and running these events. Events will include:

  • Golf Tournaments
  • Workshops and conferences
  • Roadshows
  • Exhibitions
  • Awards Ceremonies
  • Charity dinners and speaking events

These events will both showcase our work while appealing to donors to partner with us in creating opportunities.

Africa Golf Foundation is dedicated to developing the golf across the continent, with a mission to make the game more inclusive and accessible. We are breaking down barriers, creating opportunities and changing lives, and empowering entire communities. By fostering a culture of inclusion and growth, Africa Golf Foundation is paving the way for a brighter future, where golf serves as a powerful tool for personal development and community empowerment across the continent.
