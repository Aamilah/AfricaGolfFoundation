Welcome to the Africa Golf Foundation

What We Do

Dedicated to promoting and developing the sport of golf across Africa. It focuses on making golf accessible to all, especially youth and underprivileged communities, through various programs and initiatives.

About Us

The Africa Golf Foundation (AGF) is an organization dedicated to promoting and developing the sport of golf across Africa. Its goals often include increasing accessibility to golf and fostering a vibrant golfing community on the continent.

Mission Statement

To foster a vibrant golfing community that embraces all ages, genders, and skill levels, ensuring the sport's growth and accessibility throughout the continent.

Our Goals
  • Promote Golfing Excellence
  • Organize Competitions
  • Inclusive Growth
Our Purpose

The Africa Golf Foundation aims to coordinate the activities of African golfing federations to ensure the maintenance and growth of amateur golf traditions.

Who We Serve
  • Amateur and Professional Golfers
  • Golf Clubs and Federations
  • Young People and Women
  • The Wider Community

Junior Golf Development Program

The Junior Golf Development Program is designed to introduce young people to the game of golf, fostering early interest and skill development.

Golf Clinics and Camps

Regular clinics and camps where junior players receive professional coaching and participate in fun, skill-building activities.

Youth Tournaments

Age-specific tournaments that provide a competitive platform for young golfers to showcase their talents.

Scholarships and Equipment

Providing financial assistance and necessary equipment to talented young players from underprivileged backgrounds.

Women’s Golf Initiative

The Women’s Golf Initiative aims to increase female participation in golf by creating a supportive and empowering environment. As wll as improving the sports gender gap. This Program includes the following:

Women-Only Clinics

Specialized coaching sessions tailored to the needs of female golfers of all skill levels.

Networking Events

Organizing events that allow women golfers to connect, share experiences, and build a community.

Mentorship Programs

Pairing aspiring female golfers with experienced mentors to guide their development and career progression in golf.

×

Environmental Stewardship and Sustainability Program

This program emphasizes the role of golf courses in environmental conservation and sustainability, and demonstrates the power that golf has in bring a better world for generations to come. This program includes the following:

Research and Innovation

Supporting research into new methods and technologies for enhancing the environmental sustainability of golf courses.

Community Engagement

Educating golfers and the local community about the environmental benefits of golf courses and encouraging participation.

Eco-Friendly Course Management

Promoting sustainable practices in course maintenance to protect local wildlife and ecosystems.

Employment Services

The Africa Golf Foundation offers a variety of employment services to support individuals in the golfing community.

Career Counseling and Guidance

Providing personalized advice to help individuals navigate their career paths within the golf industry.

Job Placement Services

Assisting with job searches, applications, and placements in various roles related to golf, such as coaching, management, and administration.

Apprenticeship Programs

Providing access to training programs, workshops, and certifications to enhance professional skills and qualifications.

Networking Opportunities

Facilitating connections with industry professionals, mentors, and potential employers through events and online platforms.

Resume and Interview Preparation

Helping individuals create effective resumes and prepare for job interviews to improve their chances of securing employment.

Recruitment Events

Organizing events where job seekers can meet potential employers and learn about available job opportunities.

Africa Golf Foundation Annual Simpson Cup

The Ryder Cup style tournament showcases the unique position of golf as one of the few sports where players of all skills and abilities can play together on a level playing field, because of the official golf handicap system. Competitors involved in the Simpson Cup are all members of On Course Foundation USA and our sister Foundation in the UK. The event is the pinnacle of our events calendar and is our flagship event for raising awareness and funds.

Qualification Events are held in run-up to each Simpson Cup - the participant On Course Foundation members are selected based on their overall involvement with our program, their golfing ability and commitment. Captain's picks are also selected using the same criteria. For more information please refer to the Eligibility and Qualification Criteria page.
