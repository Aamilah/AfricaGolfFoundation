Empowering Youth and Talent Development

The Africa Golf Foundation is committed to nurturing young talent by providing training programs, scholarships, and opportunities for young golfers to compete in tournaments.

Promoting Inclusivity and Accessibility in Golf

AGF strives to make golf accessible to diverse communities across Africa, breaking down barriers to entry and ensuring that people from all backgrounds have the opportunity to enjoy and excel.

Enhancing Golf Infrastructure and Community

The Africa Golf Foundation invests in improving golf facilities and creating vibrant golfing communities. By partnering with AGF, sponsors can help enhance the quality and availability of golf equipment.

Our Mission

The Africa Golf Foundation (AGF) is an organization dedicated to promoting and developing the sport of golf across Africa. Its goals often include increasing accessibility to golf, supporting young and aspiring golfers, enhancing golf facilities, and fostering a vibrant golfing community on the continent.

The AGF typically engages in initiatives such as organizing tournaments, providing training programs, offering scholarships, and collaborating with local and international golf bodies to advance the sport in Africa. Their mission usually emphasizes inclusivity, youth engagement, and the overall growth of golf as a sport in African nations.

Our Partners

Get to know the companies that are behind the Afric Golf Foundation. Donate to the AFG to become a partner.

Titleist

Known for its high-quality golf balls, clubs, and gear, Titleist is a leading brand in the golf industry. They sponsor many professional golfers and are renowned for their innovation and performance.

Callaway

Another major player in the golf equipment market, Callaway offers a wide range of clubs, balls, and accessories. They are known for their cutting-edge technology and have a strong presence on professional tours.

Adidas Golf

Adidas provides golf apparel, footwear, and accessories. They are known for their stylish and functional designs, and they sponsor numerous professional golfers and events.

Cobra Golf

Known for their colorful and innovative equipment, Cobra Golf sponsors several high-profile golfers and is popular among younger players.

TaylorMade

Specializing in golf clubs, balls, and accessories, TaylorMade is known for its innovative designs and technology. They sponsor many top golfers and are a popular choice among professionals and amateurs alike.

Rolex

This luxury watch brand is a prominent sponsor of major golf tournaments and events, including The Open Championship and the Masters. Rolex is synonymous with prestige and excellence in the sport.
