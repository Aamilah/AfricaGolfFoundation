Creating Opportunities, Changing Lives, Empowering Communities

“Give a man a fish and you will feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish and you will feed him for a lifetime.”

Funding

Our grants and sponsorships are designed to assist individuals at every stage of the journey, from aspiring players, to coaches, caddies groundskeepers and officials. We also provide grants to golf clubs seeking to improve their facilities or provide access to underprivileged players, and to other organisations seeking to grow participation in the sport.

Training

We are dedicated to equipping members of Africa's diverse community with the knowledge, skills and resources needed to excel in every aspect of the sport. Through our training initiatives, we seek to empower individuals to achieve their full potential. Our comprehensive training programs cater to individuals across the golf spectrum.

Equipment and Partnerships

Providing much needed equipment to members of the golf community in Africa who are restricted from accessing the game through lack of equipment and seeking partnership opportunities for golf equipment, such as golf clubs, bags, carts, uniforms, etc to improve facilities and the manner in which the game is played in Africa.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to make golf accessible to all in Africa regardless of socio-economic background. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the joys and benefits of golf, from the camaraderie that it fosters to the lessons in discipline perseverance and sportsmanship it imparts. By providing financial assistance, mentorship programs and access to resources, we aim to empower individuals to pursue their passion for golf and realise their full potential both on and off the course.

We believe in the transformative power of golf to break down barriers and foster inclusivity. We are dedicated to providing opportunities to individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds to access and thrive in the world of golf. Whether they aspire to be players, caddies, coaches, groundskeepers, referees and officials, or simply enjoying the game at their local golf club, we are committed to levelling the playing field and ensuring that everyone has a chance to participate.

Furthermore, we recognise the vital role that golf clubs play as hubs of development and community engagement. As such, we extend our support to golf clubs across Africa, providing assistance in creating world class facilities, youth programs and capacity building initiatives.

Our Partners

Our mission to make golf accessible to all would not be possible without the support of our dedicated partners who share our vision and commitment to nurturing the next generation of golfers, fostering community engagement, and expanding the reach of this incredible sport.

We invite you to partner with us to help us fulfill our mission of fostering inclusivity, accessibility, and empowerment through the game of golf. By collaborating with us, you will play a vital role in breaking down barriers, creating opportunities, and changing lives in underrepresented communities.
