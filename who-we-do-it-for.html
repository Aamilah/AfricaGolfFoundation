Africa Golf Foundation

Removing Barriers, Creating Pathways

Our mission is to democratize the game of golf across Africa by fostering inclusivity and accessibility for all, from players and caddies to coaches, groundskeepers, referees, officials, and media workers. We strive to break down barriers and provide opportunities for individuals who have traditionally been excluded from the sport, while also supporting golf clubs in developing world-class facilities to nurture talent and foster community engagement. Through our dedication to equity and excellence, we aim to cultivate a vibrant and diverse golfing culture that enriches lives and inspires future generations.

More About Who We Serve

The Africa Golf Foundation offers a variety of services to support individuals in the golfing community. More detail can be found below:

On the Course

We provide support for every individual who sets foot on the golf course from seasoned players to aspiring players, and from coaches shaping future champions to current and aspiring caddies guiding players around the course. Our commitment extends to the unsung heroes who work tirelessly behind the scenes, from the groundskeepers maintaining the pristine greens to the referees and officials ensuring fair play and integrity.

To camera men (and ladies) capturing precious moments to the engineers who ensure that all course equipment works. We offer resources and opportunities to enhance their skills to pursue their dreams on the course and recognise and celebrate the diverse contributions of every participant on the golf course.

Off the Course

The Africa Golf Foundation is dedicated to providing comprehensive support to the growth of the sport in Africa by supporting all individuals who play a vital role in the world of golf including off the course. From fitness coaches and dieticians who optimise the player's health, to the psychologists who nurture their mental fortitude and well-being and the administrators who ensure the smooth operation of golfing events.

We are here to champion their efforts and elevate their impact. We recognise that success in golf requires a holistic approach , encompassing physical, mental and organisational support. By providing comprehensive support to off-course participants, we contribute to the overall health, professionalism and sustainability of the golf community.

Golf Facilities

The Africa Golf Foundation is dedicated to enhancing golf facilities across Africa, ensuring that players of all levels have access to world-class venues and resources to hone their sills and passion for the game. Our commitment extends to supporting a wide range of facilities, including golf clubs and golf courses, driving ranges, golf academies and research facilities , with the goal of fostering excellence, inclusivity and growth.

An investment in golf facilities is an investment in the future of the game. By supporting investment in facilities, we are creating a vibrant and sustainable golfing ecosystem that benefits players, communities and the sport as a whole. We are also dedicated to working to control global warming through partnerships with golf courses to promote sustainable methods as well as conservation initiatives. Our golf courses could be our last nature reserves and they must be maintained.

Committed to Education

We believe that enhancing skills should be complimented by education providing participants with the knowledge and tools that they require to navigate the world and pursue their dreams with confidence. We recognise that education is the foundation of success in any endeavour, and we are dedicated to ensuring that participants have access to quality education and opportunities for lifelong learning. Our educational support initiatives encompass a range of programs designed to empower individuals at every stage of their educational journey.

For students in school, we offer educational scholarships to alleviate financial barriers and provide access to quality education. These scholarships enable students to pursue their passion for golf alongside their studies. Furthermore, we provide guidance and resources to help participants access higher education whether through college scholarships, vocational training programs or pathways to continued learning.

Get Women and Girls Golfing

We will bring the transformative power of golf to every corner of Africa through innovative outreach programs. We will visit schools and communities with limited awareness of golf to raise awareness of golf and to showcase the abundance of opportunities it offers, especially for members of marginalised communities.

Africa Golf Foundation will also engage workshops and exciting events to ignite a passion for golf opening doors to new possibilities and careers in the sport and paving the way for inclusivity and opportunity in the world of golf. Part of our outreach is dedicated to promoting conservation and sustainability and our methods, literature and equipment will reflect this.

Africa Golf Foundation is dedicated to developing the golf across the continent, with a mission to make the game more inclusive and accessible. We are breaking down barriers, creating opportunities and changing lives, and empowering entire communities. By fostering a culture of inclusion and growth, Africa Golf Foundation is paving the way for a brighter future, where golf serves as a powerful tool for personal development and community empowerment across the continent.
